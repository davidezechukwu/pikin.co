It is important that we are able to positively identify our customers to comply with both regulatory requirements and to help ensure the security of accounts. We call this process KYC or Know Your Customer.KYC is a two-step process. You will need to complete one check from Step 1 and one check from Step 2. Until you have successfully completed KYC you will not be able to withdraw from your account. If you choose to upload or send in documents to complete both Step 1 and Step 2, please ensure that you use a different document in each step.

  • Upload Your Identity Document
    You can upload a copy or take a photo of your identity document by selecting one of the options below. We will add a watermark to your uploaded documents for security reasons
    • Passport
    • Drving Licence
    • ID Card
  • Upload Your Supporting Documentation (If applicable)
    You can upload any of the supporting document types specified below.
    • Utility Bill
    • Birth Certificate
